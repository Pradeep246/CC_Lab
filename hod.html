Message from HOD

Greetings! On behalf of the faculty members and students of the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, I welcome you all to the ever- great department! The Department has consistently maintained an exemplary academic record. The greatest asset of the department is its excellent infrastructure, highly motivated and dedicated faculty members. The department focuses on holistic development of the students by a combination of both curricular and extracurricular activities. We believe that to develop a tender mind we need to go beyond class room teaching and we realize that our department, which helps to make the students industry ready. We are striving hard towards the goal of providing innovative and quality education with high standard to achieve academic excellence and provides platform for the students to achieve their career goals. Our faculty members mentor and motivate the students to provide more number of solutions for making up the Digital India.National level participation in technical programs and competitions by our students adds more glory to the department. Around 30 of our students started their own company and the department strives to produce more number of entrepreneurs while they are studying in the department. Our Computer Science graduates employment rate is very high and we continue to work hard to make sure that all our graduates acquire the knowledge and skills that are essential in today’s competitive international workplace. With the blessings of the Holy God and support of the Management, I I have myself invested a great deal of time and energy in creating strong foundations for the department and will continue to do all that it takes to ensure the best job opportunities through career focused education for our students.
